About Lewis

Essex's Most Sought-After Rat Pack Tribute

Lewis Simply Swing is one of Essex's most sought-after Rat Pack tribute acts, bringing the timeless glamour of Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin and Sammy Davis Jr. to weddings, private parties, corporate events and care home performances.

With a voice and style that truly captures the spirit of the Rat Pack era, Lewis has been performing since 2012 — delivering unforgettable evenings that delight guests of all ages and ensure your event runs smoothly from start to finish.
